Simulate the 1989 or 1991 Formula One seasons via dice rolling. 
For now, they are standalone seasons, but in future you will be able to progress between seasons (with driver/team movement).
Send feedback/bug reports to F1DiceSimulator@gmail.com

V1.0.1 changelog:
* Ambulance logo showing next to suspended driver, including after their return
* History of drivers who have left teams not showing in all-time rankings
* History of teams with aliases in 1991 start (Fondmental, Footwork, Leyton House) not showing in all-time rankings or constructor's championship
* Incorrectly loading free agents

Known issues:
* Starting Pre-qualifying system for 1989 season is not fully accurate.
